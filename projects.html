Modern Home Electrical Upgrade
Project Details
  • Location: West Hartford
  • Type: Residential
  • Duration: 2 weeks
  • Year: 2023
Services Provided
  • 200-amp panel upgrade
  • Smart home integration
  • Dedicated home office circuits
  • Whole-house surge protection
  • Lighting automation
Project Description

This complete electrical system upgrade transformed a 1990s home into a modern, energy-efficient smart home. The homeowners needed additional capacity for their growing family and home office needs, along with the reliability of a fully updated electrical system.

Our team installed a new 200-amp service panel, added dedicated circuits for home office equipment, and integrated a comprehensive smart home system that allows the homeowners to control lighting, climate, and security from their mobile devices.
